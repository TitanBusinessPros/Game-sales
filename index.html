How The Commission Works

Your commission tier is determined by your total sales within a 12-month period. Once you unlock a higher tier, you maintain that rate for the remainder of that year. The cycle resets annually, giving you a fresh opportunity to climb the ladder again. There is no ceiling on what you can earn.

Example: If you reach $25,000 in sales by month 8, you immediately jump to 35% commission on all future sales for the remaining 4 months of your annual cycle.
